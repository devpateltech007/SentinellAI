2) Accomplished During This Meeting
- Learned that HIPAA compliance is an end-to-end process: policies (~30–40%), team controls and trainings, tech stack monitoring, and company-level governance — code scanning is only a small portion
- Delve integrates with GitHub using a static application code scanner with read-only access; it flags PHI leaks and vulnerabilities via inline PR comments with remediation guidance
- Discovered ISO 42001 (EU AI Act) — a framework for governing AI model usage with sensitive data; relevant since our platform uses AI with PHI/PII. Delve was one of the first 100 companies globally to achieve ISO 42001
- Learned the distinction between SOC 2 (US, NIST-based) and ISO 27001 (global/EU, ISO standard) — SOC 2 is the North America gold standard for security posture
- Delve provides pre-drafted policies (~90–95% complete on day one) that have already passed audits, drafted by CISOs and audit partners
- HIPAA does not require a third-party audit; GDPR and SOC 2 do
- Delve includes OWASP Top 10 penetration testing in their offering
- Pricing: $6,000/yr for HIPAA alone; $9,000/yr for HIPAA + GDPR (50% discount on GDPR due to control overlap)
- HIPAA compliance achievable in ~8–10 hours on Delve; adding GDPR takes ~3–4 additional hours
- Vendor risk assessments are part of the platform for evaluating third-party data handling
- Kevin mentioned that being compliant before MVP launch is a major advantage — doctor's offices won't share data without proof of compliance
